# AI Financial Co-Pilot – COULD Requirements (Innovation Layer)

## Purpose

This document defines **COULD-level features** for the AI Financial Co-Pilot.

These features represent:
- Advanced AI capabilities
- Innovation and differentiation
- Future product roadmap
- Hackathon "wow factor"

These are optional but highly valuable if implemented.

---

# 1. Conversational AI Interface (AI Chat CFO)

## 1.1 Natural Language Financial Queries

The system could provide a chat interface where the user can ask questions such as:

Examples:
- "How much did I spend on fuel this month?"
- "Who is my best customer?"
- "Why is my profit low?"
- "What are my biggest expenses?"
- "How is my business doing?"

---

## 1.2 Functional Requirements

The AI must:
- Understand natural language queries
- Retrieve relevant financial data
- Generate plain-language responses
- Use current dataset context
- Avoid accounting terminology

Example Response:
"You spent PKR 18,500 on fuel this month, which is 22% higher than last month."

---

## 1.3 Suggested Capabilities

The chat should support:
- Category queries
- Customer queries
- Trend questions
- Profit analysis
- Risk explanation

---

# 2. Proactive AI Alerts

## 2.1 Automated Insight Notifications

The system could automatically generate periodic summaries:

Types:
- Daily summary
- Weekly summary
- Monthly business report

Example:
"Weekly Summary: Revenue PKR 75,000 | Expenses PKR 52,000 | Profit PKR 23,000"

---

## 2.2 Risk Alerts

Automatically notify when:
- Large expense occurs
- Cashflow risk detected
- Revenue drops significantly
- Category spending spikes

Example:
"Alert: Unusual expense of PKR 35,000 detected for Equipment."

---

## 2.3 Delivery Channels (Optional / Mock)

- In-app notification panel
- Email summary (mock)
- WhatsApp-style message (simulated)

---

# 3. Financial Health Score

## 3.1 Overall Business Score

The system could calculate a **Business Health Score (0–100)** based on:

Factors:
- Profit consistency
- Expense ratio
- Cashflow stability
- Customer concentration
- Revenue growth

---

## 3.2 Score Interpretation

Display categories:
- 80–100: Healthy
- 60–79: Stable
- 40–59: Needs Attention
- Below 40: At Risk

Example Insight:
"Health Score: 68 – Your business is stable but expenses are increasing."

---

# 4. What-If Scenario Simulator

## 4.1 User Input Scenarios

The system could allow users to simulate changes such as:

Examples:
- Increase fuel cost by 10%
- Lose top customer
- Hire an employee
- Reduce expenses by 15%

---

## 4.2 Output

AI should estimate:
- New profit
- New expense level
- Risk impact

Example:
"If fuel costs increase by 10%, your monthly profit would decrease by PKR 4,200."

---

# 5. Goal Setting & Tracking

## 5.1 Financial Goals

Users could define goals such as:

- Reduce expenses by 10%
- Increase revenue by PKR 50,000
- Save PKR 100,000
- Achieve monthly profit target

---

## 5.2 Progress Monitoring

System should:
- Track progress over time
- Generate reminders
- Show percentage completion

Example:
"You have reduced expenses by 6% toward your 10% goal."

---

# 6. Smart Recommendations Engine

## 6.1 Cost Optimization Suggestions

AI could recommend:

- Cancel unused subscriptions
- Negotiate supplier pricing
- Reduce high-growth expense categories

Example:
"Subscription costs total PKR 12,000/month. Consider reviewing unused services."

---

## 6.2 Revenue Optimization Suggestions

Examples:
- Focus on top customers
- Identify declining customers
- Suggest pricing review

---
